What is imposter syndrome?

It is a psychological occurrence in which people doubt their skills, talents, or accomplishments and have a persistent internalized fear of being exposed as frauds [Wikipedia]

Why does it matter?

2022 study from KPMG found that 75% of female executives across industries have experienced imposter syndrome in their careers - higher among women of colour

Why are we talking about it?

  • Source of pain and shame
  • Trips people up, productivity and progress suffers
  • The fact that it's so normal means many people have solved the problem for themselves
